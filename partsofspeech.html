Parts of Speech

What is Parts of Speech?

Parts of Speech are categories of words based on their function in a sentence. Every word in English belongs to a particular part of speech.

1. Noun

A noun is the name of a person, place, animal, thing, or idea.

Example: boy, India, dog, book, honesty

2. Pronoun

A pronoun is a word used in place of a noun.

Example: he, she, it, they, we

3. Verb

A verb shows an action, state, or occurrence.

Example: run, eat, is, write, play

4. Adjective

An adjective describes or qualifies a noun or pronoun.

Example: beautiful, tall, red, honest

5. Adverb

An adverb modifies a verb, adjective, or another adverb.

Example: quickly, very, well, yesterday

6. Preposition

A preposition shows the relationship between a noun or pronoun and another word.

Example: in, on, under, between, at

7. Conjunction

A conjunction joins words, phrases, or sentences.

Example: and, but, or, because

8. Interjection

An interjection expresses sudden feelings or emotions.

Example: oh!, wow!, alas!, hurrah!
