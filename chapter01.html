Chapter 1: The Sentence

Lesson (English)

A Sentence is a group of words that makes complete sense. Every sentence must have a subject and a predicate, and it must express a complete thought.

Lesson (Manipuri / Meiteilon)

Sentence adu oiraba matamda, eina khangba nattraga khanghanbagi maram adu pumnamak piba wahei-sing amasung wahei-singgi loushing adu leite. Aduga sentence adu subject amasung predicate lei-i.

Examples

English: She goes to school every day.
Manipuri: Mahak nungsi khudol taretki matung-inna school da chat-i.
English: The sun rises in the east.
Manipuri: Numitki nungshiba nungshibi oiraba adu makha-ki theng-na thok-i.
English: I like reading books.
Manipuri: Eigi nungairaba phijol aduda eibu yar-i.
